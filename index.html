Our Story

A Few Moments

It began quietly, the way the best things sometimes do. We crossed paths at university — never quite speaking, but always noticing, each of us holding a small, unspoken admiration for the other. Then the years took us our separate ways. We graduated in 2018 and didn’t meet again until late 2024, when a single Instagram friend request reopened a door we hadn’t realized was still ajar. A message, then another, and slowly we found our way back to each other. By October 2025 the conversations had deepened — one long phone call that neither of us wanted to end — and somewhere in all of it, the truth simply slipped out: every time I looked at her, I felt certain I would marry her. "Then I should start calling you my husband," she said. "I can’t wait to make that a reality," I told her. We meant it.

For a while, love lived across the distance between Lagos and Abuja — two cities, two lives, one steady pull toward each other. In December 2025 we finally met again, and what had been admiration became something whole and undeniable. We’ve since learned that our favourite moments aren’t the grand ones, but the quiet ones: simply being in each other’s presence, needing no words at all. On the 29th of May, 2026, a question was asked and a forever was promised. And now, surrounded by the people we love, we begin the rest of it together.
